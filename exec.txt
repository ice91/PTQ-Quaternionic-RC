4. Algo-server 上可直接執行的四條命令
假設 algo-server 上的 repo 路徑為 ~/PTQ-Quaternionic-RC 且 .venv 已建立。
(1) 啟動六模型 baseline 的 nohup 指令（Layer A）
nohup bash scripts/run_baseline_sixmodels_12000.sh \
  > results/revision_baseline_sixmodels_12000/logs/run_baseline_sixmodels_12000.nohup.out 2>&1 &
cd ~/PTQ-Quaternionic-RCnohup bash scripts/run_baseline_sixmodels_12000.sh \  > results/revision_baseline_sixmodels_12000/logs/run_baseline_sixmodels_12000.nohup.out 2>&1 &
(2) baseline 跑完後做 aggregate summary 的命令
cd ~/PTQ-Quaternionic-RCsource .venv/bin/activatepython scripts/summarize_baseline_sixmodels.py
輸出會寫到：
results/revision_baseline_sixmodels_12000/aggregate/
(3) 啟動三模型 multi-seed robustness audit 的 nohup 指令（Layer B）
revision_r
cd ~/PTQ-Quaternionic-RCnohup bash scripts/run_robustness_core_multiseed12000.sh \  > results/revision_robustness_core_multiseed12000/run_robustness_core_multiseed12000.nohup.out 2>&1 &
(4) robustness 跑完後做 aggregate summary 的命令
cd ~/PTQ-Quaternionic-RCsource .venv/bin/activatepython scripts/summarize_robustness_core_multiseed.py
輸出會寫到：
results/revision_robustness_core_multiseed12000/aggregate/
